SECTION 12: QUESTIONS TO ASK THE INTERVIEWER
==================================================================

1. "What does the day-to-day work of this role look like? Is it more new feature development, maintenance, or a mix?"

2. "What hardware or devices will I be writing drivers for? Are there existing datasheets and reference designs available?"

3. "How is the team structured? Are driver developers and application developers on the same team, or separate?"

4. "What does the code review process look like for kernel-mode code? Given the consequences of bugs, how do you ensure quality?"

5. "What's the biggest technical challenge the team is currently facing?"

6. "How do you handle driver testing? Do you have hardware-in-the-loop test infrastructure?"

7. "What does the onboarding process look like for someone new to driver development?"

8. "What operating systems and kernel versions do you target? Do you support both current and LTS versions?"

9. "How do you handle driver signing and certification? Do you go through WHQL certification?"

10. "What debugging tools are commonly used by the team — WinDbg, KGDB, something custom?"

11. "How do you handle the transition from development to production for drivers? What does your staging environment look like?"

12. "What's your deployment/update process for drivers in the field? Is there an over-the-air update mechanism?"

13. "How do you balance the stability required for driver code with the need to ship new features?"

14. "What does success look like in the first 90 days for someone in this role?"

15. "How does this team approach cross-functional collaboration — with firmware, hardware, and software application teams?"

16. "Is there an opportunity to contribute to open-source driver work, or is everything proprietary?"

17. "What C++ standard does the team use (C++11, C++17, C++20)? Are there coding standards in place?"

18. "How does the team handle breaking changes in the Linux kernel API between versions?"

19. "What's the culture around learning and professional development here? Is there a budget for conferences, training, or certifications?"

20. "What tools do you use for project management and tracking (Jira, Azure DevOps, GitHub Projects)?"

21. "How do you measure the impact and quality of driver code? What metrics matter to the team?"

22. "Is this role more individual-contributor focused, or is there a mentoring/technical leadership component?"

23. "What's the team's philosophy on technical debt? How do you balance new development with code quality improvements?"

24. "How has this role evolved over the last few years, and how do you see it evolving?"

25. "What do people on the team enjoy most about working here? What do they find most challenging?"
